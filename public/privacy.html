Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 20, 2026

My Weekly Plan helps you manage tasks and weekly schedules. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Account information (for example, email address) when you sign up or sign in.
  • Planner content you create, such as tasks, notes, links, due dates, and schedule details.
  • Basic technical information needed to operate and secure the service.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and maintain your account and planner data.
  • Sync your tasks across sessions and devices.
  • Improve reliability, performance, and security of the app.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services for authentication and hosting, including Supabase, Vercel, Google, and Meta (Facebook Login), depending on the sign-in method you choose.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers required to run the app.

Data Retention

We retain your account and planner data until you request deletion or your account is removed.

Your Rights

You can request access, correction, or deletion of your data by contacting support@myweeklyplan.com.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to support@myweeklyplan.com.
