User Data Deletion

Last updated: February 20, 2026

You can request deletion of your My Weekly Plan account and associated data at any time.

How to Request Deletion

  1. Email support@myweeklyplan.com from the email on your account.
  2. Use subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  3. Include your account email and request confirmation once complete.

What We Delete

  • Your account profile data.
  • Your planner tasks, notes, links, attachments metadata, and schedule data.

Deletion Timeline

We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days. Some limited records may be retained where required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.

Questions

Contact support@myweeklyplan.com for help with deletion requests.
